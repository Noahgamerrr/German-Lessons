Der Dativ ist dem Genitiv sein Tod

(The dative is the death of the genitive)

27th May 2021

What does that mean?

Well, it means that in certain areas in Germany and all over Austria, the construction "von + dative" has partially or even fully replaced the genitive case. That has the effect that in those regions, there are only three cases instead of four.

Examples:

English: The man's car is green.

Standard German: Das Auto des Mannes ist gr체n.

Austrian German: Das Auto vom Mann ist gr체n.

English: The list of the players isn't complete.

Standard German: Die Liste der Spieler ist nicht vollst채ndig.

Austrian German: Die Liste von den Spielern ist nicht vollst채ndig

This grammatical change can sometimes be confusing, especially on headlines. In Austria, a headline like: Mutter von drei Kindern ermordet can have two possible meanings:

  1. Mother of three children was killed.
  2. Mother was killed by three children.

(Even though it should be quite clear through context that it is the former.)

The German headline, however, can only be interpreted in one way: Mutter dreier Kinder ermordet (The second interpretation would be constructed the same way as the Austrian one)
